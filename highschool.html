neuqua valley consultants

Neuqua Valley Consultants is a club still in development. Currently, with the help of some of my friends, we are bringing to Neuqua the first club that will attempt to mimic the quantitative and qualitative analysis that consulting firms engage in to transform and/or improve the performance of companies, which includes finance, people, operations, and technology. We will employ, as mentors and coaches, individuals who have devoted their life to the career of consulting.
