instruments

cello

I started playing cello in the 5th grade, but I only started playing competitively when I came to high school. Currently, I play for Chamber Strings, the highest orchestra at my school and have played for the Illinois State Orchestra through the Illinois Music Educator's Association.

Favorite piece: Lalo Cello Concerto in D Minor

piano

When people ask me what my favorite instrument is, it is hard for me to answer the question. Often times I find myself choosing between the cello and the piano; however, there is a certain sound and passion I have for the piano that always has me leaning towards it as my favorite instrument. It is an extremely versatile instrument, that in my opinion, is easier to pick up than other instruments. I started playing piano when I was about 6 years old have not stopped since. I play music from all genres, jazz to classical to rock.

Favorite piece: United States of Eurasia by Muse (integrates Chopin's Nocturne in E-flat major, Op. 9, No. 2 beautifully)

vocals

I can't say I'm a professional vocalist, nor can I say that I am a "good" singer. But the fact of the matter is that, you don't need to be good at something to love it. Singing for me is usually a stress reliever; there's nothing I enjoy more than going to the piano and wailing away while I play the reptitive cadance of Clocks by Coldplay.

Favorite piece: Clocks by Coldplay
